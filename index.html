01 — Profile

Presence, framed in grayscale.

I'm Ahnaf Miftahu Al Muntasib, from Bogura, Bangladesh — this is a personal archive of moments shot without color, where mood does the talking. Every frame here trades saturation for silence: skies, silhouettes, and the honest grain of a phone camera at dusk.

I study at APBn Public School & College, SSC batch 2028. The work leans into contrast, negative space, and stillness — no filters chasing trends, just light behaving the way it wants to.
