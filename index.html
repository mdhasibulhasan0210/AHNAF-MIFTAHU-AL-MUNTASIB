01 — Profile

Presence, framed in living color.

I'm Ahnaf Miftahu Al Muntasib — this is a personal archive of moments where color does the talking. Every frame here trades silence for saturation: skies on fire, silhouettes against neon dusk, and the honest grain of a phone camera chasing the light.

The work leans into contrast, negative space, and stillness — but never shies from a bold hue. No filters chasing trends — just light behaving the way it wants to.
